Milestones

Our team carefully divided the project into the following specific, realistic, achievable goals.

  • Goal A

    Find Key Words

    Allow users to upload videos, translate speech-to-text, and allow users to search for specific words.

  • Goal B

    Jump Through Videos

    Allow users to easily jump through a video by clicking on the outputted search results.

  • Stretch Goal

    Add Login Capabilities

    Allow users to log in, save, and see previously uploaded videos.

  • Current Team

    Progress

  • Future Work

    Identify Filler words

    Google and Assembly AI speech-to-text APIs ignore repetitive phrases and filler words. Thus, this goal has become a stretch goal. In the future, we would like to create a speech-to-text AI model and leverage it to identify filler words.
